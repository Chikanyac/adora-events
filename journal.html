Journal & Notes — Selected
Note 01 — Lighting

Light that flatters, not blinds

We use warm 2700K, bounced off linen. No direct spots on faces. Every table gets its own pool of light.

Note 02 — Portions

Considered, not scarce

Portions calibrated to ritual: lobola requires abundance, weddings require pace. We design for both.

Note 03 — Protocol

Culture held correctly

From munyayi to speeches, we learn the order before we propose the menu. Respect first, then creativity.

Note 04 — Sound

Rooms should hum, not shout

Our playlists are edited like scent — low, warm, familiar enough to make elders nod.
